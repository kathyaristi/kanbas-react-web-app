How to make pancakes:
  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients.
  3. Stir to combine.
  4. Heat a skillet or griddle.
  5. Pour batter onto the skillet.
  6. Cook until bubbly on top.
  7. Flip and cook the other side.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
